Section 5 – Duties of the Librarian 

   The librarian is charged with storing and preserving, as much as possible, all
   printed material and software items that are club property, including copies 
   of all Disks-of-the-Month.

   The purpose of the software library is to provide a source of quality software
   to the members of the GCCC. 

   The library shall be governed under the copyright laws of the United States of
   America.

   The GCCC Library shall be for the use and benefit of members only. 

   The Librarian may publish a list of available software in the club newsletter 
   at no charge. 

   The Software Librarian may charge members for reasonable duplication and 
   mailing expenses, but may not otherwise charge for access to the GCCC Library.
