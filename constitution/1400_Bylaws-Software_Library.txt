Software Library

   General

     The purpose of the software library is to provide a source of quality
     software to the members of the GCCC.

     The software library shall be governed under the copyright laws of the
     United States of America.

   Operation

     The GCCC Software Library will be for the use and benefit of members only.

     With approval of the Board of Directors, the Disk of the Month Director
     may appoint a Software Librarian, otherwise the Disk of the Month
     Director is the Software Librarian.

     The Software Librarian shall publish a list of available software in the
     periodical at no charge.

     The Software Librarian may charge members for reasonable duplication and
     mailing expenses, but may not otherwise charge for access to the GCCC
     Library.
