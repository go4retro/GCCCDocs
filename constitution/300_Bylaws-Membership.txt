Membership Categories

   The membership of the GCCC shall consist of the following categories:

     General Membership

     Contributing Membership

     Honorary Membership

   General Membership is open to anyone in the Community.

   Contributing Members in good standing are entitled to one vote at General
   Meetings of the GCCC.

   A Contributing Member shall be deemed to be in good standing upon payment
   of the annual Membership dues of the GCCC.
